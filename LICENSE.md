**Spider-Man 3** is an open-world action-adventure game based on the iconic Marvel superhero and the popular film adaptation. Players step into the role of Peter Parker as he faces personal struggles, powerful enemies, and the mysterious influence of the alien symbiote.

The game features an expansive recreation of New York City, allowing players to freely explore the city, complete missions, stop crimes, and uncover secrets while mastering Spider-Man's incredible abilities.

## 🕹️ Gameplay Overview

In **Spider-Man 3**, players freely traverse New York City using Spider-Man's signature web-swinging abilities. Along the way, players can complete story missions, rescue civilians, stop crimes, and confront notorious villains.

The addition of the **Black Suit** introduces a darker side to Spider-Man, granting powerful attacks and unique abilities while influencing the storyline and gameplay experience.

The game blends exploration, combat, and storytelling to deliver an authentic superhero adventure.
